# MC Meal – Soda Sprint MVP v1

Third mini-game in the MC Meal retro arcade style.

## Gameplay

- Move the soda nozzle left/right.
- Hold Space / FILL to pour soda into moving cups.
- Stop in the green perfect zone.
- Overfilling or bad fills cost lives.
- Perfect pours build combo and improve rewards.

## Progression

10 levels:
1. Classic Soda
2. Double Pour
3. Fizzy Rush
4. Night Refill
5. Golden Soda Bar

Each level increases:
- cup count
- cup speed
- spawn rate
- smaller perfect fill zone

## Rewards

Demo reward summary:
- Soda
- Sauce
- Fizz Combo
- XP
- Mystery Order Ticket chance
- Rare Recipe Fragment chance


## v2 Easier Mode Changes

- 5 lives instead of 3

- fewer cups per level
- slower pour speed for better control
- wider nozzle hit area
- bigger good/perfect fill window
- less punishing overfill
- fewer cups on screen at once
