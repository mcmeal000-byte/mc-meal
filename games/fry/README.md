# MC Meal – Fry Rush MVP v1

Second mini-game in the MC Meal retro arcade style.

## Gameplay
- Move the tray left/right.
- Catch fries, sauce, nuggets and golden fries.
- Avoid burnt food and kitchen rats.
- Build combos for higher score and better rewards.
- Space / BOOST gives a short speed boost.

## Progression
10 levels:
1. Fry Starter
2. Sauce Rush
3. Double Fry
4. Night Shift
5. Golden Fry War

## Rewards
Demo reward summary:
- Fries
- Sauce
- Combo Spice
- XP
- Mystery Order Ticket chance
- Rare Recipe Fragment chance
